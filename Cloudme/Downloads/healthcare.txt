Health care or healthcare is the maintenance or improvement of health via the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of disease, illness, injury, and other physical and mental impairments in people. 
Health care is delivered by health professionals (providers or practitioners) in allied health fields.
 Physicians and physician associates are a part of these health professionals. 
Dentistry, midwifery, nursing, medicine, optometry, audiology, pharmacy, psychology, occupational therapy, physical therapy and other health professions are all part of health care.
 It includes work done in providing primary care, secondary care, and tertiary care, as well as in public health.
